Register new voter

Register with email, Aadhar and mobile. Mobile number must be verified via OTP on this page before account creation.

Why verify mobile?

In a real system, OTP over SMS prevents fake accounts and ties one voter to one phone number. Here OTP is simulated on screen for learning.

Flow:
1. Enter mobile → Send OTP
2. Check the simulated OTP text below the field
3. Enter OTP → Verify OTP
4. Register. If mobile or OTP changes, verification must be done again.

After successful registration, you can login normally with email and password, or via Aadhar OTP.
